Welcome
The beautiful church dates back to the 4th century, although most of the building was constructed between the 10th and 12 centuries. You just can't miss the bronze door, with 48 elaborately-carved panels of scenes from the Bible, dating back to the 11th century. Something pretty cool about this church is the fact that the bell tower is 62 m high and not even attached to the church!
